Terawatt Industries specializes in open source 3D Printers, especially RepRaps, and is the home of the PrismX Open-Source 3D Printer. We fulfill several open source parts for 3D printers and DIY electronics. Follow the links below to view our open-source 3D things. STL viewers, download links, and documentation are available. Note: Github now has builtin STL viewers so you can browse any of our machines here and in the repository.
